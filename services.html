Our Services

Global B2B Sourcing & Technical Support

We identify and qualify the right suppliers for your needs, bridging the gap between clients and factories to deliver complete products from concept to delivery. Our services cover supplier audits, technical specification reviews, prototype validation, production monitoring, packaging compliance, and logistics coordination. Built for B2B clients worldwide, we provide cost-effective, reliable, and genuine solutions — ensuring quality, transparency, and on-time delivery across every stage of your supply chain.

Factory Audit

On-site supplier evaluation covering production capacity, quality systems, certifications, and workmanship standards.

Sample Validation

Prototype and pre-production testing to ensure product specifications, materials, and performance meet your requirements.

Supplier Sourcing

Identifying and qualifying reliable manufacturers based on your product type, budget, and quality expectations.

Production Monitoring

In-line quality control during manufacturing to catch issues early and keep production on schedule.

Inspection Service

Final random inspection before shipment with detailed reports, photos, and pass/fail recommendations.

Logistics Management

Shipping coordination, customs clearance, and door-to-door delivery management from factory to your destination.

Technical Support

Spec review, BOM optimization, compliance verification, and design-for-manufacturing guidance.

Sourcing Portal

Online platform for RFQ management, supplier comparison, order tracking, and document sharing.
